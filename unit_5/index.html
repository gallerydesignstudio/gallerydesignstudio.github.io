Services

GDS is a graphic design studio based in Long Island City, New York. We provide a range of graphic services from logos to marketing collateral design. Our services are not just about making things look good, we use design to attract, inform and connect clients to your brand via multiple platforms.

Logo Design

We create professional logos to ensure that your business gets noticed. Your brand delivers a clear promise to customers and your logo will be their first impression.

Style Guides

Consistency is quality. Having a professional logo is a great start, however applying a consistent style across your business’s materials portrays quality and credibility. Here at Gallery Design Studio we provide easy-to-follow style guides that will typically include logo variations and correct usage, font choice, color schemes, layouts and imagery choice.

Marketing Collateral Design

At GDS we design compelling marketing collateral that supports the sales of your product or service. Some examples of what we do are: ad units, brochures, business cards, catalogues and flyers.

Digital Design

GDS also provides graphic design services for digital use. Some examples of what we do are: digital catalogues, newsletter design and social media artwork.

Photography Services

Your business’s imagery plays a key role on setting the tone and conveying brand values. Here are at GDS we can take care of your photography needs, whether it’s on location (New York City metropolitan area) or in a studio setting.

Color Consulting

Color is a powerful tool used to communicate an emotion and can sometimes signal subliminal feelings that no one notices. Here at GDS our trained color consultant can correctly identify which color scheme works best for your brand and will understand how to achieve harmonious and innovative combinations.
