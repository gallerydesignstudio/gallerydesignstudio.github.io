Services

We are a graphic design studio based in Long Island City, New York. Our services are not just about making things look good, we use design to attract, inform and connect clients to your brand.

Marketing Collateral Design

We design compelling marketing collateral such as ad units, brochures and catalogues that support your company’s sales.

Digital Design

GDS provides graphic design services for digital use like digital catalogues, e-newsletters and social media artwork.

Style Guides

Applying a consistent style across your business materials portrays quality and credibility. GDS provides easy-to-follow style guides to help you look professional.

Photography Services

Your business’s imagery plays a key role on setting the tone and conveying brand values. Here are at GDS we take care of your photography needs, whether it’s on location (New York City metropolitan area) or in a studio setting.

Color Consulting

Color is a powerful tool used to communicate emotions. Our trained color consultant can correctly identify which color scheme works best for your brand and will understand the psychology to achieve harmonious combinations.

Logo Design

We create professional logos to ensure that your business gets noticed. Your brand delivers a clear promise to customers and your logo will be their first impression.

Our team

Caroline Petersen

Graphic Designer

Caroline is responsible for the creation of design concepts for online, offline, digital media, print and other assigned projects. She has prior experience in managing marketing campaigns for online boutiques and has acquired training at two London-based food magazines, mainly assisting with shoots for videos and stills. Caroline holds a Graphic Design Certificate from the Shillington School NY and a Business Administration degree, majoring in marketing, from San Pablo CEU University.

Mike Yamin

Photographer

Mike is a photographer with 10+ years experience in the field. He started his career at a luxury portrait studio in Connecticut and almost immediately began taking on weddings as well. After assisting two prominent commercial photographers, he decided to expand and was soon doing portrait and product photography for local and national clients. Today, Mike often photographs people, food, and real estate. Some of his latest work can be seen in Threads and Fine Cooking magazines.

Meghan Fitzpatrick

Color Consultant

Meghan holds a Professional Development Certificate from FIT Color Specialist/Image and Style Concentration. She works with emerging consumer brands developing color palettes. She also holds a B.A. in French and Italian from Villanova University. Meghan's latest work can be seen at “green start-up” growwithroot.com where she developed a color palette that captured the essence of the brand.
